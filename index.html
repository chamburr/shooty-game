About

Shooty Game is a simple shooter game made with p5.js. This game will bring you the joy you never know you could feel. After the developer built the game, he played it for 24 hours straight! This game was created for a Computer Studies project in school.

Features

Shoot Frustration Away

Are you full of frustration after a day of school or work? Don't wait further - open fire and shoot all your frustration away! You'll instantly become energetic like never before.

Play with Friends

Play this amazing game with your friends and spend quality time together. Don't have friends? That's okay too - you can play in single-player mode against our super intelligent robot.

Improve Concentration

Fix your concentration or procrastination problem once and for all. Immerse in Shooty Game until 10pm and you'll instantly become productive. Don't believe us? Try it out yourself!

Easy to Learn

Don't like complex controls? You've found the right game then! With minimal controls that are super easy to learn, you can get into intense gameplay within minutes.

Enjoy Anywhere

Play this game wherever you are, as long as you have a computer. The game is cached locally the first time you open it, and you can play it afterwards even without internet!

Open Source

Shooty Game is an open source project hosted on GitHub. You can customise the game to your liking to improve your playing experience, or contribute to make the game better!

Be immersed now!

Dive into our favourite Shooty Game now! Have fun and enjoy.

Warning: You might not be able to stop playing.
