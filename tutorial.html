Tutorial

Learn how to play Shooty Game under 5 minutes and enjoy the game for rest of your life.

  • Single-player
  • Multi-player

Step 1

Open a browser and enter the link https://chamburr.github.io/shooty-game/play.html.

Step 2

Select single-player mode by clicking the button.

Step 3

Use WASD keys to move, VB keys to change the direction of the gun.

Step 4

Enjoy the game! Get 5 points to win.

Step 1

Open a browser and enter the link https://chamburr.github.io/shooty-game/play.html.

Step 2

Select multi-player mode by clicking the button.

Step 3

Player 1: Use WASD keys to move, VB keys to change the direction of the gun.
Player 2: Use ↑←↓→ keys to move, ./ keys to change the direction of the gun.

Step 4

Enjoy the game! The first player to get 5 points wins.
